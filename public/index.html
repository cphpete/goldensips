Espresso Martini Bold & Velvety
A decadent cocktail combining the boldness of espresso with the smooth sweetness of coffee liqueur and vodka. Perfect for those who enjoy rich, layered flavors.
Origin: Invented in 1983 by bartender Dick Bradsell at the Soho Brasserie in London, England.

Recipe

  • 4 cl Vodka
  • 3 cl Kahlua
  • 5 cl Freshly brewed espresso
  • 0.5 cl Licor 43

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a martini glass.
