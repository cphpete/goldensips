Espresso Martini
Bold & Velvety
A decadent cocktail combining espresso and coffee liqueur.
Origin: London, 1983
  • 4 cl Vodka
  • 3 cl Kahlua
  • 5 cl Espresso
  • 0.5 cl Licor 43
  • 0.5 cl Tequila Coffee Liqueur
Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.
